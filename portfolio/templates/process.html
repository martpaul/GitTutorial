The most important of design is not just the final product but each and every step that goes along with it. From sketching to brainstorming to research to of course design, it's key to iterate through each to produce a better product.

BRAINSTORM

There are better things in life than sitting in a room and throwing ideas on a white board. Before every project it's important to generate every possability from every viewpoint. By working with other designers, users, friends, etc. nearly every persceptive can be dervived.

SKETCH

I am a cronic doodler. Anywhere there's a white space and time I am drawing out something. Either the next great idea, the future of interfaces, or a demon filled dungeon to send my DnD group through I am generating ideas from brainstorms to papers.

RESEARCH

Numbers have been my main focus for a while. From working with the National Science Foundation to the NYC Metropolitan Transit Service my research experience is far reaching. I understand the importance of numbers and effectivly using them to tell a story.

DESIGN

Design is not just the cummulation of the process but is done at every step of the way. From finetuning the kerning of a font, picking the right hue, or removing all chart junk it's the little details that count.
