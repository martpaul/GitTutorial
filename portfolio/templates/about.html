ABOUT

I am a former data analyst turned UI/UX designer. I am interesting utilizing paper sketches and protoptypes to better come to grips with design problems. The study of fonts and their use in minimalistic design is of importance to me.

After a life of numbers I have decided to apply my research skills towards another field where I can flex my creative muscles. I am a first year masters student at the University of Michigan where I am studying Human Computer Interaction. By combing my love of design, quanatative / qualatative research, and technology I am able to hit every synapse in my brain for maximum fullfillment.

_____

EDUCATION

I am currently obtaining my Masters at the University of Michigan in Human Computer Interaction with a focus on User Interaction. Many moons ago I grduated from the Rochester Institute of Technology where I studied Statistics, Economics, and History.

EXPERIENCE

In my life I have wore many hats. From car factories to janitorial work to doing research for the National Science Foundation and the National Oceanic Atmospheric Agency I know how to deal with all situations and people. I am excited for new experiences, especially those that place me out of my comfort zone. Interaction Design is currently doing just that.

I have extensive use of statistical programs like R, Tableau, SAS and SPSS. I am quasi fluent in the Adobe Suite. I have used and loved computers since they have had only four colors.
